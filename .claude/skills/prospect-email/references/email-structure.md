# Email Structure — Prospect Email Agent

Anatomy rules, subject line formulas, CTA types, and generic templates per ICP.
The Prospect Email Agent reads this before drafting any email.

---

## Universal Rules

- **Max 150 words for cold/SPARSE leads.** Shorter = higher reply rate.
- **Max 200 words for RICH/PARTIAL leads.** More context earns more length.
- **Never start with "I hope this finds you well."** Never.
- **Never lead with a feature list.** Lead with their world, then your relevance to it.
- **One CTA only.** Two asks = zero replies.
- **Plain text.** No HTML, no bullets, no bold. Reads like a real email from a real person.
- **Sign-off:** Always `Gian` + `Co-Founder, Prometheus iQ`
- **Always address the contact by first name** (from Contact Name field in ClickUp).

---

## Email Anatomy

```
Subject: [specific — see formulas below]

Hi [First Name],

[Opening hook — 1 sentence]
Reference: their world, a pain, a context, or the prior relationship.
Never start with "I" — start with them.

[Value bridge — 1–2 sentences]
Connect what they're dealing with to what Prometheus iQ does.
Tie it to their specific context (PMS, property count, pain from call notes).

[CTA — 1 sentence]
One clear ask. Low friction. Easy to say yes to.

[Sign-off]
Gian
Co-Founder, Prometheus iQ
```

## Subject Line Formulas

Use these as starting points, not fill-in-the-blank templates. Adapt to the lead's context.

| Situation | Formula | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Known pain point | `[Their pain] — one fix` | `Pricing 40 properties manually — there's a faster way` |
| PMS-specific | `[PMS] + revenue intelligence` | `Built for Guesty operators like you` |
| Competitive angle | `What your comp set is doing right now` | `What your comp set is doing right now` |
| Re-engagement | `Since we last talked —` | `Since we last talked — a lot has changed` |
| Property count | `[N]-property operators are doing X` | `30-property operators are pricing differently now` |
| Generic / no data | Short, curious, easy | `Quick question about your pricing setup` |

**Subject line rules:**
- Under 50 characters when possible
- No exclamation marks
- No "RE:" unless it's a real reply thread
- Specific > clever

## Personalization Injection Points

When you have RICH or PARTIAL data, inject personalization at these points:

| Inject Point | What to use | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Subject line** | Their PMS, pain point, or property count | Custom fields |
| **Opening hook** | Call note detail, a specific thing they said, prior relationship | Task comments |
| **Pain reference** | Their BANT "what problem are you solving" answer | BANT section |
| **Value bridge** | Map their PMS to Prometheus iQ integration | PMS field |
| **CTA** | Their timeline from BANT | BANT → Timeline |
| **Tone** | Calibrate to their sales stage | Sales Stage field |

The goal: every RICH lead should feel like Gian wrote this specifically for them after reviewing their notes — because that's exactly what happened.
